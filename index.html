Chicken Adobo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken thigh or drumstick
  • 2 lemons, juiced (about ¼ cup juice)
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ mama sita's sauce
  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 1 head garlic, peeled and minced
  • add 5 eggs
  • 3 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 cup water
  • salt to taste

Intructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate your chicken thigh with soy sauce and garlic for 30 minutes or an hour depending on what you chose, and make sure to massage it.
  2. After marinating, prepare all the ingredients in one place
  3. In a pan medium heat, heat oil. Add the sliced onion and garlic. And then add the chicken and cook for 3 to 5 minutes and wait until it becomes brown, and then add ¼ cup of soy cause.
  4. Add ¼ cup of soy cause and then the mama sita's sauce. Wait for 5 minutes and then serve!
